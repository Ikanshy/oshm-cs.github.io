Refund, Cancellation, Shipping, and Delivery Policy for Oshm

Refund Policy

Eligibility for Refunds: Customers are eligible for a refund if they receive incorrect or damaged products.

Process for Requesting a Refund: To request a refund, please contact us at oshm@ikanshy.com within 24 hours of pickup, providing details of the issue along with proof of purchase.

Refund Processing: Eligible refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original method of payment.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Period: Orders can be cancelled up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time.

How to Cancel: To cancel an order, use the cancellation feature in the Oshm app or contact customer service.

Cancellation Fee: Orders cancelled after the specified period may incur a nominal cancellation fee.

Shipping Policy

Shipping Locations: As Oshm facilitates local pickups, shipping to customer locations is not applicable.

Shipping Charges: There are no shipping charges, as Oshm does not offer home delivery services.

Delivery Policy

Pick-Up Locations: Customers will receive a notification with the Kirana store's location for order pickup.

Pick-Up Time: Customers are expected to pick up their orders within 24 hours of receiving the pickup notification.

Failure to Pick Up: Orders not picked up within the specified timeframe may be automatically cancelled, and a restocking fee may apply.

Contact Information

For any queries regarding refunds, cancellations, or pickups, please contact us at oshm@ikanshy.com.
